CRITERIA FOR CONSIDERATION OF PALLIATIVE CARE CONSULT-CMS GUIDELINES

Condition

Criteria

Heart Disease

-Patient optimally treated with diuretics and vasodilators

-Significant or recurrent symptoms of CHF(Class IV on NYHA Scale)

-Patient has inability to carry on any activity without SOB or discomfort

 

Supportive History:  MI, Arrest, Syncope, CVA, Recurrent Super ventricular arrhythmias

Cancer

-Any metastatic disease that is inoperable or aggressive treatment is complete

Pulmonary Disease

-Disabling dyspnea at rest,  unresponsive to bronchodilators, resulting in decreased functional capacity.(bed to chair existence)

-Progression of end stage disease as evidenced by increasing visits to the MD office, ER visits, or 2-3 admissions to the Hospital in 1 Yr

-Patient has hypoxemia on room air with 02 saturations less than 88 percent on oxygen.  PC02 greater than 50%

 

Supportive History:  Patient has Right Heart Failure, Wt loss greater than 10% of body weight over 6 months.  Resting Tachycardia

Cerebrovascular Accident

Acute Phase

-Patient is in coma or persistent vegetative state for more than 3 days

-Patient is obtunded beyond 3 days past the event

-Patient has dysphasia and is not receiving artificial nutrition

Chronic Phase

-Patient has post stroke dementia with the following

-Stage 7 or higher on FAST scale        Unable to ambulate w/o assist

-Unable to perform ADL’s w/o assist  Incontinence of Bowel/Bladder

-No meaningful verbal communication  Karnofsky of 40% or less

Dementia

-Inability to walk with out assistance

-Stage 7 or higher on FAST scale

-Requires assistance with ADL’s

-No consistent meaningful communication, just repetitive, canned phrases

Last 12 Months: (One of the following) Required Tx for pneumonia, UTI, Septicemia, Stage 3-4 Decubitus, Fever recurrent after treatment. Unable to maintain sufficient nutritional intake.

Failure to Thrive

-Frequent ER visits, MD visits or Acute Admissions

-Albumin less than 2.5g/dL

-Unintentional weight loss     -Recent decline in functional status

-Dependence on ADL care     - Decreased nutritional intake

Renal Disease

-Not a candidate for dialysis

-Creatinine clearance less than 15 mL/min

-Serum creatinine level greater than 8.0

Liver Disease

-Patient may be on transplant list and still receive hospice

-Refractory ascites, Jaundice, Malnutrition and muscle wasting

HIV Disease

-Patients most recent CD4 count must be < 25 cells/mcL)

OR  Viral load greater than 100,000.

-Must have at least one:  Lymphoma, not responsive to Tx, Wasting, MAC bactremia, Kaposi sarcoma unresponsive to tx, renal failure, toxoplasmosis
